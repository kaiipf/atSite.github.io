Why choose us?

Art Thematic stands out by combining all art activities into a cohesive study flow, guiding students from visual art creation to model making, and finally, to storyboard development. This hands-on approach is further enriched with STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics) discovery projects, designed to enhance independent thinking and problem-solving skills.

Our program aims to inspire the next generation of innovative leaders by cultivating a mindset that values creativity, exploration, and critical thinking. Whether your child dreams of becoming an architect, a scientist, or a storyteller, Art Thematic provides the tools and inspiration to help them realize their full potential.

Enroll your child today and watch them thrive in a learning environment that bridges the gap between art and science, nurturing both their imagination and intellect!

Our programs

ArThematic's unique programming explores a new topic each month, ranging from exciting topics in history, geography, science and many more. Each month, children engage with these subjects through a structured approach built on three core pillars: Thematics, Inspiration and Innovation. This approach ensures a rich, well-rounded learning experience that sparks curiosity, encourages creativity, and fosters innovative thinking.

Thematic Month

During Thematic Month, we focus on integrating drawing, crafting, storyboarding, and presenting into a unified study flow, with our goal being to guide children through the creative transformation of a themed idea into a fully realized project, whilst also learning about the world around them. Throughout this process, your child will explore various aspects of creative design, ultimately mastering skills such as visual illustration, 3D modeling, and effective storyboarding for presentations. By the end of the program, they will have developed a comprehensive project that showcases their creativity, technical skills, and storytelling abilities.
